About

Whether you love sports or simply have an interest in how some of the biggest legends got to where they are today, there is a wealth of wisdom to be learned from any sports book. That’s why we’ve worked to create this specially curated list of the top titles on the market right now, as we recognise how valuable it is for people to learn from those who have pushed their bodies – and minds – to the limit.

Our top ten list has been carefully compiled after hundreds of hours spent reading and researching everything the genre has to offer. The results have come after our expert team compared reviews and thoughts on their favourite titles and created the ultimate selection which would give any reader a whistle stop tour of some of the most memorable names and moments in the world of sport.

A range of sports has been covered, from cricket to basketball, with a mixture of perspectives and personalities gathered together in one comprehensive list. We hope you enjoy reading this top 10 list as much as we enjoyed making it.
